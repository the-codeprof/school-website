About us

We are IT training, project management, web design & development & consultancy company in Lagos, Nigeria, specializing in today’s most in-demand skills.

CodeProf Computer Academy is Nigeria’s best Indigenous IT Training Establishment. We have excelled in IT Training/Education, Publishing, IT Consultancy and IT Solutions Development & Services. We have trained over 400 students at our IT Training Centre located in Lagos and via our online training platform. Businesses and individuals can choose from hundreds of course offerings, delivered by experts, and offered in our training center or via our online platform. For many small, medium and large organizations, CodeProf Computer Academy is the single source for all their IT training needs.

Mission Statement

The mission: To open the minds of students to specialised knowledge by imparting quality education in Information Technology; to provide state-of-the-art technology and infrastructure to our students; and to enable them to build rewarding careers in IT.

Also to contribute immensely to the socio-economic development of Nigeria by empowering individuals or corporate bodies with the rightful IT skills needed to boost their career opportunity. Provide the citizens with IT Training Solutions in the contemporary Information and Communication Technologies at “value for money” prices so that Nigeria progresses rapidly to emerge at par with the developed economies of the world

Vision Statement

The school aims to build and sustain a comprehensive academic institute which values service through high quality education in information and communications technology.

Also our vision is to be that ICT institution that offer a high-quality practical training on various ICT skills, as well as to prepare students for career opportunities in this area requiring a high level of technical knowledge and skills. Codeprof Computer Academy will continue to develop and administer programs which serve the needs of industrial computer scientists, researchers, and computer application specialists for whom the discipline of computing sciences provides indispensable tools.
